Flood

A flood is an overflow of water that submerges land that is usually dry. They can cause widespread destruction and are among the most common natural disasters.

Fire

Fire disasters can spread quickly and cause severe damage. Knowing fire safety procedures can save lives and prevent property destruction.

Earthquake

Earthquakes are the shaking of the Earth's surface caused by sudden energy release in the crust. They can be extremely destructive and occur without warning.
